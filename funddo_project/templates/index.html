Recent Transformations

Gift Hope

Many job-seekers in underserved communities can barely afford transportation to interviews let alone a haircut and grooming services. With your help, they can focus their limited funds on getting to interviews.

Change Lives

Fund someone’s do today, and help them look their best for their interview. A one time donation of your services can lead to possible employment for them and long-term customer/advertiser for your establishment.

Increase Confidence

Dressing professionally can be expensive, but when you have a great haircut or hairdo you feel confident no matter what you’re wearing. Funding hair or grooming services can help take some pressure off of job-seekers who can’t buy their ideal interview outfit.
